English Tenses Quizzes

  • Present Simple Tense

    10 Question

    The present simple tense is used to express general truths, habits, and routines. It is also used in instructions, commentaries, and scheduled events.

  • Past Simple Tense

    10 Question

    The past simple tense is used to describe completed actions in the past. It often involves specific time expressions and is commonly used in narratives and storytelling.

  • Future Simple Tense

    10 Question

    The future simple tense is used to discuss actions that will happen in the future. It is often used with time expressions and predictions.

  • Present Continous Tense

    5 Question

    The Present Continuous Tense is used to express an action or event that is currently ongoing and not yet completed at the present moment.

  • Past Continous Tense

    5 Question

    The Past Continuous Tense is used to express an action or event that was ongoing in the past when another event occurred.
